### 2. Federal Bonding Program
**Administering Agency:** U.S. Department of Labor via Illinois Department of Employment Security
**Jurisdiction Level:** Federal w/ State Interface  
**Program Type:** Risk Mitigation
**Status:** Active
**Target Populations:** Justice-impacted individuals, recovering substance abusers, welfare recipients, economically disadvantaged youth, individuals with poor credit records
**Brief Description:** Provides free fidelity bonding insurance to employers who hire at-risk job applicants. Bonds cover the first six months of employment and protect employers against employee dishonesty (theft, forgery, larceny).
**Maximum Value Per Employee:** $25,000 policy limit (insurance coverage, not cash to employer)
**Key Employer Eligibility Requirements:** All employer types eligible, must provide full-time employment (30+ hours/week)
**Geographic Trigger:** Work site location matters (YES), Candidate home address matters (NO), Employer HQ location matters (NO)

**Confidence Level:** High - established federal program
